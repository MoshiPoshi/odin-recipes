A delicious Lasgna straight from the oven

Description

Lasagna is the ultimate comfort food, layers of rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and tender pasta sheets all baked together until golden and bubbling.
This classic Italian dish is hearty, indulgent, and guaranteed to impress at any table.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds ground beef
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 3 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese, grated
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Bon appetit!
