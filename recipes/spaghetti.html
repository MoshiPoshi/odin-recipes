A hearty Spaghetti Bolognese the whole family will love

Description

Spaghetti Bolognese is a timeless Italian classic — tender spaghetti topped with a slow-cooked, deeply savory meat sauce that gets better the longer it simmers.
Simple, satisfying, and endlessly comforting on any night of the week.

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Parmesan cheese for serving

Bon appetit!
