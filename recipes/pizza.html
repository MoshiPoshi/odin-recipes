A classic Margherita Pizza fresh out of the oven

Description

Margherita Pizza is the purest form of pizza — a crispy thin crust topped with rich tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella, and fragrant basil leaves.
Proof that the best things in life are always the simplest.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough ball
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 200g fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Semolina flour for dusting

Bon appetit!
